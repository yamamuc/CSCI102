    Babel's List of "How to stay Alive":
  1. Violence isn't alway the answer! Try to see if the creatures before want to negotiate.
  2. Stay away from monsters! You are most vulnerable in short-range combat.
  3. Reserve your spell slots! Try to use only what you need, UNLESS a person is unconscious.
  4. If a person is unconscious, disregard rule 2 and help them as quickly as possible. Casting Healing Word is the
    best option if you're a Cleric.
  5. Use the environment to your advantage! Traps, pits, and other dangers can be used against your enemies!

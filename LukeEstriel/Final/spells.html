There are many spells that you can cast. While it is recommended to look over available resources,
book or otherwise, allow me to provide some of my personal favorites spells for the three casters
I mentioned prior: Cleric, Sorcerer, Wizard.

Before that, let me explain "Spell Slots"

You cannot cast magic forever. Magic is like a wallet full of coins, once you use them you use them.
Spell Slots can range from level 1 to 9. The higher the number, the greater the effect.
For now, I shall offer you knowledge of the simplest of spells: level one spells.
Here is a list of some spells for each spellcaster type:

Class Name Spell Number 1 Spell Number 2 Spell Number 3
Cleric Bless Healing Word Protection from Evil and Good
Sorcerer Absorb Elements Burning Hands Magic Missile
Wizard Absorb Elements Mage Armor Shield

Here is another table with simple descriptions of each spell I just listed.

Spell Name Description
Absorb Elements Reduce incoming Acid,Cold, Fire, Lightning or Thunder damage by half.
Bless Three creatures (including yourself, if you want!) add a d4 to all attacks rolls and saving throws they make.
Burning Hands Project a cone of fire, burning everyone in its range.
Healing Word Heal a creature (including yourself, if you want!) for a d4 plus your spellcasting modifier (for Clerics this is your Wisdom) .
Mage Armor Increase your Armor Class (AC), making you harder to hit.
Magic Missile Three bolts of magic automatically hit a target, each doing 1d4+1 Force damage.
Protection from Evil and Good Certain monsters will have a more difficult time attacking or influencing you with their effects.
Shield Temporary increase your Armor Class (AC) by 5 until the start of your next turn.

Do note these are not the specific wording for these spells - it is best to read the actual wording to
prevent confusion. Once again, you can find such paragraphs in printed books or online. However,
searching the names of the spells (Such as the ones below) can be a good place to start.
